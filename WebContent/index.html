Rectangle Position Relation

Each rectangle is represented by the left bottom coordinate and right top coordinate. Each coordinate value is separated by comma (no space in between), and each coordinate value is non-negative integer.

In order to determine the position relation of two rectangles, please enter the left bottom coordinate and right top coordinate of the rectangle into each blank below. And then click on "Determine Position Relation" button. Results will show below "Position Result".

For example, for Rectangle 1 with left bottom coordinate of (0, 1) and right top coordinate of (3, 5), please enter 0,1,3,5 into the blank under "Rectangle 1".

To re-enter rectangle coordinates, please click on "Reset" button.
